Cultural Etiquette
When visiting Marrakech, bargaining in the souks is secondary to mutual respect. Start with a friendly "Salam Alaykum" (Peace be upon you), ask for prices respectfully, and enjoy the bargaining process as a lively, polite social interaction. Modest dress is appreciated, particularly around historical neighborhoods.
